My name is Yaël Ossowski.

I’m a consumer and technology advocate, writer, syndicated radio host, podcaster, and sometimes digital entrepreneur based between Vienna, Austria and North Carolina.

I’m Deputy Director at the Consumer Choice Center, and a contributor to newspapers, magazines, and online outlets on issues of technological innovation, lifestyle freedom, and smart policy. And itcoin.

Since 2023, I'm a Visiting Fellow at the Bitcoin Policy Institute, where I'm hosting much of my research and writing on state-level public policy on Bitcoin.

I'm a co-host on the syndicated radio show and podcast Consumer Choice Radio, and a collaborator and co-host on the Fix The Money podcast and Substack.

You can read my 10+ year archive of articles, follow me on social media, or contact me via email or PGP.
